Stock management software made with C#. Pretty straight forward project for adding and deleting items, tracking sales and so on.

Apart from the usual features, I have also added a permission system so that only certain type of users control the certain parts of the system. For instance if you are a salesman on the system you can only sell products, you cannot do anything else like buying or changing the price of a product.

There are total of 5 job titles and each has it's own unique role on the system.
