Third design of the website made with Foundation framework.


At first I didn't want to use any CSS frameworks because they lack customizability and uniqueness, they made every website look the same.


But after I struggled to find a new, unique concept, I decided to give CSS frameworks a chance. Tested out various different frameworks, from the most mainstream ones to unheard ones.


And I finally decided to stay on Zurb Foundation It has the best maintainability and stability compared to other frameworks not counting the mindblowing documentation quality.


Apart from maintainability part there is not much things to say about this design.
