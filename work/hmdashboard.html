Proof of concept dashboard made with UAS, DCG and Halfmoon framework.


GitHub: Source


After I managed to put UAS and DCG in a stable state, I wanted to test both of them in a project. At the same time there was a front-end framework that I really wanted to use, which was Halfmoon .

One of the key feature of the Halfmoon that I've mentioned in the past is, that it was made for making dashboards. With that in mind, making a dashboard was the perfect way to test out the capabilities of the projects.

In the making of the dashboard, I really liked the agnostic structure of the UAS. I only have to fiddle with a single table for assigning a permission to an user. The only thing that made me hesitate was the Fat-free framework dependency, which I only used for routing for the AJAX requests. I'm planning to remove it from the project as soon as possible.

I have also realized that the DCG needs a much more polishing than I've previously thought. I've changed the render priority, removed some useless pieces of codes and changed the way of how templates are stored and rendered.

Even though DCG is currently more stable than ever, these changes didn't satisfy me enough so I have come to a conclusion that I need to rewrite the whole DCG in a more concise way. This is my next goal for the 2022.
